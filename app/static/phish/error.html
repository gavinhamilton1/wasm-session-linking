What is Smart Log On


Smart Log On is the new way to quickly and securely log on to your WorkspaceVDI with your personal device.

Using the Smart Log On mobile application, registered users can log on by either scanning the QR code or sending a push notification directly to their registered device.

Users are then able to authenticate using their mobile devices fingerprint reader or face id.

Requirements

  • Mobile device with the Smart Log On application installed and registered
  • Biometrics (fingerprint or face ID) enabled on your device
